Departments

Our departments cover a wide spectrum of medical fields, ensuring comprehensive support for all your health needs.

  • Cardiology
  • Neurology
  • Orthopedics
  • Obstetrics and Gynecology (OB/GYN)
  • Radiology

Cardiology

The Cardiology department focuses on diagnosing, treating, and preventing diseases of the heart and blood vessels. Cardiologists manage conditions such as coronary artery disease, heart failure, arrhythmias, and hypertension.

Services:

. Electrocardiograms (EKGs)
. Echocardiograms
. Stress tests
. Cardiac catheterization
. Angioplasty and stent placement

Neurology

Neurology deals with disorders of the nervous system, which includes the brain, spinal cord, and peripheral nerves. Neurologists treat conditions such as epilepsy, stroke,multiple sclerosis, Parkinson's disease, and migraines.

Services:

. Neurological examinations
. Electromyography (EMG)
. Electroencephalography (EEG)
. Lumbar punctures
. Neuroimaging (MRI, CT scans)

Orthopedics

Orthopedics focuses on the musculoskeletal system, including bones, joints, ligaments, tendons, and muscles. Orthopedic surgeons treat conditions such as fractures, arthritis, sports injuries, and spinal disorders.

Services:

. Joint replacement surgery
. Arthroscopy
. Fracture repair
. Physical therapy
. Sports medicine

Obstetrics and Gynecology (OB/GYN)

The OB/GYN department focuses on women's reproductive health, pregnancy, and childbirth. Obstetricians care for pregnant women and deliver babies, while gynecologists manage female reproductive health issues.

Services:

. Prenatal care
. Labor and delivery
. Gynecological exams
. Pap smears
. Family planning and contraception

Radiology

Radiology involves imaging techniques to diagnose and treat diseases within the body. Radiologists interpret medical images and may also perform interventional procedures.

Services:

. X-rays
. Computed Tomography (CT) scans
. Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI)
. Ultrasounds
. Interventional radiology procedures
